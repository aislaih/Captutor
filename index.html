About Cap-Tutor


STEAM vs. STEM

Cap-Tutor is an interactive teaching toy that allows students age 8-12 to explore different concepts of STE(A)M Education through storytelling. The main component of the device is a camera and an internet connection. The student is able to use this device by taking pictures of their work in progress from beginning to end. Using all the images collected from the devic, the student will be able to display their process on a webpage in an engaging manner.

How does it work?


Using the Cap-Tutor Camera

Step 1: turn the camera on by pressing on the switch

Step 2a (optional): place the camera on the mounting platform to get optimal results

Step 2b: point the Camera to the desired position

Step 3: press the spinner like you would a button to take a picture

Step 4:repeat steps 2 and 3 until satisfied with amount of pictures

Viewing the pictures as a stop motion animation

Step 1: connect to the local network

Step 2: connect to Cap-Tutors IP address by typing the following in the address bar: 192.168.4.1/

Step 3: click on the “try me button” to launch the app

Step 4a: select a desired resolution

Step 4b: Click Capture to take another picture

Step 4c: click View Images to view the images in a slide show manner

Step 5: turn the knob to navigate between the images
